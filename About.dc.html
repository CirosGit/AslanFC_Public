About the club

More than a club. A movement.

Aslan FC is a soccer club based in Central Florida dedicated to developing talented players and creating opportunities for athletes to reach their highest potential.

Our mission

Building more than a club — a legacy.

Founded on the principles of excellence, discipline, integrity, and community, Aslan FC provides a competitive environment where players can grow both on and off the field. Our goal is not only to compete at the highest levels of the U.S. soccer pyramid, but to build a sustainable pathway for future generations through a comprehensive youth academy.

We believe every player deserves the opportunity to develop their talent, pursue their dreams, and become a leader within their community. Through professional coaching, player development programs, and a commitment to excellence, we are building a legacy — where talent is developed, character is built, and opportunities are created.
